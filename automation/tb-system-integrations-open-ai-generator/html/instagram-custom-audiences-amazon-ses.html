Integrating Instagram Custom Audiences with Amazon SES

As businesses aim to reach a larger audience, they are utilizing various marketing strategies, two of which are Instagram Custom Audiences and Amazon SES. Instagram Custom Audiences allows businesses to create a custom audience based on specific criteria and target them with ads. Amazon SES, on the other hand, is an email marketing service that enables businesses to send emails to their customers and subscribers.

Integrating the two services is beneficial as it allows businesses to create and send targeted emails to their Instagram Custom Audiences, leading to a higher engagement rate and ROI. This integration can be achieved through an API or SDK.
