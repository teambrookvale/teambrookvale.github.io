Webhooks by Zapier and Zoho CRM Integration

In the world of digital business, staying updated about important activities taking place on your website or application is critical. Traditional methods of monitoring only allow you to get updates on limited events. That’s where webhooks come in. Webhooks by Zapier are a modern way of automating the delivery of crucial event notifications to other useful applications like Zoho CRM.

Zoho CRM is an easy-to-use sales CRM software that can automate day-to-day sales tasks, automate workflows and help in analyzing customer data. The key to getting the most out of your CRM is integrating it with other applications like webhooks.

Integration of Webhooks and Zoho CRM

Integrating webhooks and Zoho CRM is a simple and straightforward process. You can achieve this through API or SDK. API allows you to send data from one application to another. SDKs are software development kits that provide developers with access to libraries and tools to build custom integrations.

Zapier is an application that enables you to connect webhooks with Zoho CRM through hundreds of pre-built integrations. Zapier operates by creating workflows containing triggers (webhook events) and actions (what happens when the webhook is triggered). It’s easy to set up and gets the work done efficiently.
