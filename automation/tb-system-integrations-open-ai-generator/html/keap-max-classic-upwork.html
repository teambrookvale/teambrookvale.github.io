What is Keap Max Classic?

Keap Max Classic is a marketing automation platform that helps small businesses automate their sales and marketing workflows. It provides features such as email marketing, lead management, appointment scheduling, and invoicing. Keap Max Classic also has a CRM system that helps businesses track their customers' interactions and manage their sales pipeline.

What is Upwork?

Upwork is a freelancing platform that connects businesses with talent all over the world. It provides access to a range of freelancers, from web developers to graphic designers and more. With Upwork, businesses can outsource certain tasks to experts in their field, saving them time and money.

Integration of Keap Max Classic and Upwork through API or SDK

The integration of Keap Max Classic and Upwork can be achieved through API or SDK. With API integration, businesses can automate certain workflows, such as creating new contacts in Keap Max Classic when a freelancer is hired on Upwork. SDK integration allows businesses to build custom integrations between the two platforms based on their unique needs.
