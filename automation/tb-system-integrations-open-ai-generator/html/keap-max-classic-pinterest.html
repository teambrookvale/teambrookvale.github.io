What is Keap Max Classic?

Keap Max Classic is a CRM and marketing automation software that helps small businesses automate their marketing and sales activities. It offers a range of features, including email marketing, lead scoring, and appointment scheduling. Keap Max Classic also allows businesses to manage their customer database, track their sales activity, and automate their follow-up process.

What is Pinterest?

Pinterest is a visual discovery and bookmarking platform that allows users to discover and save ideas for their projects and interests. As a social media platform, Pinterest is an ideal place for businesses to showcase their products and services to potential customers. With over 459 million monthly active users, Pinterest is a valuable platform for businesses that want to reach a wider audience and increase their sales.

Integration of Keap Max Classic with Pinterest

By integrating Keap Max Classic with Pinterest, businesses can streamline their marketing efforts and improve their workflow. This integration can be achieved through an API or SDK, which allows businesses to easily pull data from Pinterest and add it to their Keap Max Classic account. Some of the benefits of integrating these two platforms include:

  • Automatically adding new Pinterest followers to Keap Max Classic database
  • Creating automated email campaigns based on Pinterest activity
  • Tracking Pinterest engagement data in Keap Max Classic
