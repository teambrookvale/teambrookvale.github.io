How Integrating BigCommerce and Namely Can Significantly Improve Your E-commerce Business

As the e-commerce industry continues to grow, businesses need to search for solutions that can help them stay competitive in the market.

Two such solutions that have garnered significant attention recently are BigCommerce and Namely. While BigCommerce is an e-commerce platform that allows businesses to create online stores within minutes, Namely is an all-in-one HR management system that helps businesses manage payroll, benefits, and HR compliance in one centralized location.

When these platforms are integrated using APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) or SDKs (Software Development Kits), they can help businesses immensely by solving the challenges they face in their day-to-day operations.

Integration of BigCommerce and Namely through API or SDK

Integration of BigCommerce and Namely can be completed using APIs or SDKs. Both platforms provide documentation and support for developers to ensure a seamless integration experience.

Using APIs, businesses can ensure a real-time flow of information between the two platforms, which eliminates the need for manual data entry and reduces the likelihood of data errors.

Alternatively, SDK integration allows businesses to create their own customized integrations that meet their specific needs, while providing the flexibility and control required to manage the integration process.

Problems Their Integration Solves

Integration of BigCommerce and Namely can solve several problems that e-commerce businesses face regularly. These problems include:

Managing HR Information:

With Namely, businesses can manage all HR information in one place, including payroll and benefits. Integration with BigCommerce makes it easy to track employee sales commissions and bonuses, ensuring accurate payment to employees.

Eliminates manual data entry:

Integration ensures a real-time flow of information, eliminating the need for manual data entry, reducing the likelihood of data errors, and freeing up valuable time that can be used for other tasks.

Improves employee productivity:

With automated processes in place, employees can divert their efforts to critical tasks such as building relationships with customers, improving the customer experience, and increasing sales.

Conclusion

By integrating BigCommerce and Namely, businesses can improve their efficiency, increase their sales, and focus on the core components of their operations. Both platforms have documentation and support systems that make integration quick and easy.

Regardless of the size of your e-commerce business, integrating these two platforms can prove to be a valuable investment. Not only can they help you streamline your operations, but they can also help you manage HR information more efficiently.
