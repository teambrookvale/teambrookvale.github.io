Keap Max Classic

Keap Max Classic is an all-in-one CRM, sales, and marketing automation software that helps small businesses grow. With features such as lead capture, appointment scheduling, email marketing, and invoicing, you can manage your customer interactions and grow your business efficiently. Keap Max Classic also offers integrations with other tools to further enhance your business processes.

Greenhouse

Greenhouse is a modern recruiting software that helps companies find and hire the best talent. With features such as job postings, candidate tracking, interview scheduling, and analytics, Greenhouse makes the hiring process more efficient and effective. Greenhouse also offers integrations with other tools to further streamline your recruiting process.

Integration of Keap Max Classic and Greenhouse through API or SDK

With the integration of Keap Max Classic and Greenhouse through API or SDK, you can automate your recruiting and sales processes. When a candidate applies for a job through Greenhouse, Keap Max Classic can automatically create a new lead in the CRM. This will help you keep track of all your candidates in one place and make it easier for your sales team to follow up with them. Similarly, when a lead becomes a customer in Keap Max Classic, Greenhouse can automatically update the candidate's profile to reflect their new status as a customer. This will help you keep your candidate and customer data consistent across both platforms.

Problems their integration solves

The integration of Keap Max Classic and Greenhouse through API or SDK solves several common problems that organizations face:

  • Manual data entry: With the integration, you no longer need to manually enter candidate or customer data into both platforms. This saves time and minimizes the risk of errors.
  • Disparate systems: By integrating Keap Max Classic and Greenhouse, you can keep all your candidate and customer data in one place. This makes it easier to access and analyze data, and provides a more complete picture of your business.
  • Poor communication between teams: When candidate and customer data is stored in separate systems, it can be difficult for your sales and recruiting teams to stay on the same page. With the integration, both teams have access to the same data, which improves communication and collaboration.
