How Amazon Seller Central and Namely Integration Streamlines E-commerce Business Operations

Amazon Seller Central is a platform that offers a wide range of services to sellers, including order and inventory management, real-time data analytics, and customer support. Namely is a cloud-based HR solution that provides payroll, benefits management, employee record keeping, and compliance management services. While these two software solutions may seem like they serve different purposes, integrating them via API or SDK can bring tremendous advantages for e-commerce businesses.

Integration of Amazon Seller Central and Namely

Integrating Amazon Seller Central and Namely can be done using an API or SDK. By integrating the two platforms, sellers or business owners can seamlessly manage their employee payroll, benefits, and HR records while monitoring their sales, orders, and inventory in real-time.

Problems their Integration Solves

Integrating these two platforms solves several problems for e-commerce businesses, including:

Mistakes in Payroll Processing

Integrating Amazon Seller Central and Namely eliminates errors that can occur in payroll processing and tracking of employee time, attendance, and benefits.

Manual Data Entry

With the integration, manual data entry is reduced or eliminated, leading to faster and more accurate order processing, inventory management, and HR-related tasks.

Improved Employee Experience

The integration of Amazon Seller Central and Namely enables employees to access their pay stubs, benefits plans, and other HR-related information directly from the Amazon Seller Central portal. This streamlines the employee experience and makes it easier for them to access important information.

Conclusion

Integrating Amazon Seller Central and Namely using an SDK or API is a smart move for e-commerce businesses. This integration can lead to improved payroll processing, reduced manual data entry, and a better employee experience.
