What are Webhooks by Zapier?

Webhooks are automated messages sent from web applications to other applications when an event occurs. Webhooks by Zapier is a service that listens to these messages and triggers an action in another application. For example, when a new lead is added to SugarCRM 7+, Webhooks by Zapier can send an email notification to your team or create a new task in your project management tool.
