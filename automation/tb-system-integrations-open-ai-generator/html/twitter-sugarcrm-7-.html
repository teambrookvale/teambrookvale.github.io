Integrating Twitter and SugarCRM 7+ through API or SDK

Integration of Twitter and SugarCRM 7+ enables businesses to capture valuable customer insights, monitor social media activity, engage with customers in real-time, and create targeted campaigns. With the integration, businesses can track customer behavior, preferences, and conversations on Twitter and use this data to develop products and services that meet their customers' needs.

API (Application Program Interface) and SDK (Software Development Kit) are two popular integration methods that technical teams can use to integrate Twitter and SugarCRM. API simplifies the process of accessing specific features or data from one application to another. In contrast, SDK provides all the necessary tools and libraries for building native apps or custom integrations.

Problems their integration solves

  • Slow response time: Whether it's answering customer inquiries or responding to tweets, businesses that take a long time to respond risk losing their customers to competitors. Twitter's real-time conversations provide a platform for businesses to offer immediate support and provide timely solutions to customer queries.
  • Low engagement: With millions of tweets being posted every day, keeping track of relevant conversations can be challenging. Integrating with SugarCRM 7+ provides businesses with an easy way to monitor and engage in conversations without having to go through multiple tweets manually.
