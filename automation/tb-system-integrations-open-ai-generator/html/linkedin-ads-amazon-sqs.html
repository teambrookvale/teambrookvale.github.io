Integration of LinkedIn Ads and Amazon SQS for a Better Advertising Experience

LinkedIn Ads is a great platform for businesses and marketers to reach out to their desired audience professionally. Amazon Simple Queue Service (SQS) is a fully managed message queuing service that helps in decoupling and scaling microservices, distributed systems and serverless applications. By integrating the two via API or SDK, you can benefit from the best of both worlds, solving the following problems:

  • Effective Targeting: LinkedIn has a huge database of professional users and Amazon SQS provides a means to streamlining message processing for targeted ads based on the user's behaviour and interests.
  • Reliable Message Delivery: With Amazon SQS, advertisers can be assured that their ads will be delivered in a reliable and timely manner.
  • Scalability: Amazon SQS has the ability to handle high traffic campaigns, which LinkedIn Ads may not be able to without having an impact on the network performance.

Developers can use LinkedIn's Marketing Developer Platform to access LinkedIn's Ads API and then integrate it with Amazon SQS using the SDKs (Software Development Kit) available. The entire process can be made easier and scalable by utilizing AWS Lambda, Amazon API Gateway, and Amazon S3 buckets.

Conclusion

The integration of LinkedIn Ads and Amazon SQS through the API/SDK approach presents many benefits for businesses and marketers. It enhances the effectiveness of advertising strategies, provides reliability in delivery and scalability for high traffic campaigns. With the right development tools and technologies, the integration process can be made seamless and easier for developers. By taking advantage of these two platforms, advertisers can reach out to their target audience with confidence and achieve better ROI.
