What are Facebook Lead Ads?

Facebook Lead Ads are a form of advertising where businesses can capture leads or customer information directly on Facebook. When a Facebook user clicks on a business's ad, a form will be displayed that they can fill out without ever leaving Facebook. The information they provide is then sent directly to the business.
