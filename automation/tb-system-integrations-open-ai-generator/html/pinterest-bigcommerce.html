Integration of Pinterest with BigCommerce

Pinterest

Pinterest is a social media platform that allows users to discover, save and share images and videos by collecting them on virtual boards. It has over 250 million monthly active users and is a powerful tool for businesses looking to market their products visually.

BigCommerce

BigCommerce is a leading ecommerce platform that enables businesses to create and manage their online stores. It powers over 50,000 online stores and has features such as customizable templates, payment gateways, inventory management and shipping options.

Integration of the two through API or SDK

The integration of Pinterest with BigCommerce allows businesses to connect their online store with their Pinterest account. This integration can be achieved through APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) or SDKs (Software Development Kits).

The APIs or SDKs enable the businesses to display their products on their Pinterest boards and allow customers to click on the products and be directed to the relevant product pages on the BigCommerce store. This integration enables businesses to target customers who are specifically interested in their product categories and increase sales conversions.

Problems their integration solves

Without the integration of Pinterest with BigCommerce, businesses may have to manually upload their product images on their Pinterest boards, which can be time-consuming and inefficient. It may also lead to discrepancies in product information displayed on Pinterest and the BigCommerce store, causing confusion for customers.

The integration helps to streamline the marketing process and ensures that product information remains consistent across platforms. It also enables businesses to reach a wider audience and may lead to increased sales.

Conclusion

The integration of Pinterest with BigCommerce is a valuable tool for businesses looking to enhance their online presence and increase sales conversions. The integration can be achieved through APIs or SDKs, and it helps to streamline the marketing process and keep product information consistent across platforms.
