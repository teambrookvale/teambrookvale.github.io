Pinterest and ServiceNow: A Match Made in Integration Heaven

If you're familiar with Pinterest, you know it's a social media platform where users can save, or "pin," images and videos to virtual boards. ServiceNow, on the other hand, is a cloud-based platform that offers a wide range of services to help businesses manage their operations.

But what do these two platforms have in common? The answer is simple: they can be integrated through API or SDK.

What is API and SDK integration?

An API (application programming interface) is essentially a set of rules that allows different software applications to communicate with one another. An SDK (software development kit) includes the tools developers need to create an application that can access the API of another platform.

Why integrate Pinterest and ServiceNow?

At first glance, it may not seem obvious why a business might want to integrate these two platforms. But there are actually a number of benefits:

  • Efficiency: By integrating Pinterest with ServiceNow, businesses can automate the process of creating work orders based on the pins employees save. For example, if an employee saves a pin of a new product they'd like to test, this could trigger a work order for the testing team to begin their evaluation.
  • Centralization: Many businesses use Pinterest to collect inspiration for everything from marketing campaigns to office decorations. By integrating with ServiceNow, the information on these pins can be organized and accessed in one place, streamlining the creative process.
  • Collaboration: With an integrated Pinterest and ServiceNow system, employees can share pins with one another and collaborate on projects more effectively.

Conclusion

While it may seem unconventional to integrate a social media platform with an enterprise management system, Pinterest and ServiceNow actually complement each other quite well. By using APIs or SDKs to integrate the two platforms, businesses can increase efficiency, centralize information, and improve collaboration among employees.

If you're interested in integrating Pinterest with ServiceNow, do some research into the API or SDK options available. With the right development team on your side, the possibilities of an integrated system are endless.
