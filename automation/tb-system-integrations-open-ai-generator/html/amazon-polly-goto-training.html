Amazon Polly and GoTo Training: A Winning Combination for eLearning

The rise of eLearning has opened the doors for businesses and educational institutions around the world to provide flexible and accessible training to their employees and students. However, creating engaging and high-quality learning content can be a challenge. That's where Amazon Polly and GoTo Training come in. In this post, we'll explore how these two powerful tools can be integrated through API or SDK, and how this integration solves some common eLearning problems.

What is Amazon Polly?

Amazon Polly is a cloud-based text-to-speech service that uses advanced deep learning technologies to synthesize natural-sounding speech from plain text. It can be used to convert written content such as documents, articles, and even entire books into audio files for listening on-demand. Amazon Polly supports a wide range of languages and voices, and offers a variety of customizable settings to fine-tune the output.

What is GoTo Training?

GoTo Training is a popular online training platform that allows trainers and instructors to create interactive and engaging learning sessions for their participants. It offers features such as screen sharing, video conferencing, polling, and more, to help trainers deliver their content effectively. GoTo Training is also available on mobile devices, making it easy for learners to access the training content from anywhere, at any time.

Integration through API or SDK

By integrating Amazon Polly with GoTo Training, trainers can enhance their training sessions with natural-sounding voiceovers and audio descriptions. This not only helps to make the content more engaging, but also improves the accessibility for learners with visual impairments or those who prefer audio content. The integration can be achieved through either API or SDK, depending on the requirements and level of customization needed.

Problems their integration solves

Integrating Amazon Polly and GoTo Training can solve several common eLearning problems, such as:

  • Creating high-quality audio content quickly and easily
  • Improving engagement and retention rates among learners
  • Providing accessibility for learners with visual impairments
  • Enhancing the overall learning experience and perceived value of the training

Conclusion

By combining the power of Amazon Polly and GoTo Training, trainers and instructors can take their eLearning content to the next level. With natural-sounding voiceovers and audio descriptions, they can create more engaging and accessible training experiences for their learners, while also saving time and effort in the process. Whether through API or SDK, the integration is a win-win for everyone involved.
