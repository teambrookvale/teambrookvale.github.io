The Power of Instagram Custom Audiences and Integration with Namely

Instagram has grown to become one of the largest social media platforms in the world, with over 1 billion active users every month. It’s no surprise that businesses have begun to focus on Instagram as a potential marketing tool to reach their target audiences. However, with so many users on the platform, it can be challenging to identify and reach the right people. That's where Instagram Custom Audiences can help.

What Are Instagram Custom Audiences?

Instagram Custom Audiences are lists of people who have interacted with your brand in some way. These interactions could include website visitors, people who engaged with your social media profiles, or customers who have purchased from you in the past. By grouping these people together, you can create targeted ads that are more likely to resonate with them.

Integration with Namely

Namely is an HR and payroll platform that helps businesses manage their employees’ information, from onboarding to payroll. By integrating Namely with Instagram Custom Audiences, you can create more targeted ads based on your employees and potential employees' profiles and behavior on Instagram. This integration is facilitated through the use of APIs and SDKs.

Problems Solved by Integration

The integration between Namely and Instagram Custom Audiences solves several problems that businesses might encounter when marketing on the platform:

  • Limited targeting options: Without Instagram Custom Audiences, businesses can only target audiences based on basic demographics like age, location, and gender. The ability to target specific people who have engaged with your brand allows for more precise targeting.
  • Lack of personalization: By grouping audiences based on their behavior and engagement with your brand, you can create ads that are more personalized and relevant to your audience.
  • Difficulty creating effective ads: By using Namely’s employee data, you can create ads that speak to the interests and needs of your specific audience on Instagram.

Conclusion

Integrating Namely with Instagram Custom Audiences allows businesses to create more targeted ads that are more likely to resonate with their audience on the platform. By using audience data from Namely, businesses can create personalized ads and increase the effectiveness of their marketing efforts on Instagram.
