Instagram lead ads are advertisements that collect information from people interested in your product or service. Namely

Namely is a human resources management software that offers businesses a way to streamline their HR and payroll processes. Namely's platform includes tools for managing employee information, benefits, time off, and more. With the integration of Namely, businesses can easily manage and track the leads generated through their Instagram lead ads.
