How Integration of Google Ads and SugarCRM 7+ through API or SDK Solves Your Business Problems

Marketing and sales teams in enterprises use numerous tools to manage their campaigns and leads. Google Ads is one of the popular platforms, and SugarCRM 7+ is among the leading CRM software. Integrating the two gives teams a lot of benefits, including better insights and streamlined workflows.

What are Google Ads and SugarCRM 7+?

Google Ads is Google's advertising platform that allows teams to run online campaigns across websites and apps. SugarCRM 7+ is a comprehensive customer relationship management tool that includes sales, marketing, and customer support features.

Integration through API or SDK

The integration of Google Ads and SugarCRM 7+ is possible through either an API or an SDK. While the API enables the transfer of data between the two platforms, the SDK goes further to offer extra functionalities that can enhance the integration. With the integration, sales and marketing teams can access data, such as campaign performance and customer behavior, from both platforms.

Problems the integration solves

Integration of Google Ads and SugarCRM 7+ creates a seamless environment where users can manage their campaigns from within SugarCRM 7+. The integration offers three primary benefits:

1. Improved Campaign Management

The integration makes it easier for users to create, monitor, and optimize campaigns in real-time. Users can view their Google Ads campaigns and make changes to ad copies, targeting, and budget directly from within SugarCRM 7+. The integration eliminates the need to switch between platforms, saving time and reducing errors.

2. Better Lead Management

When a lead submits a form on a website or clicks on an ad, a record is created in SugarCRM 7+ that contains information such as their name, contact details, and what attracted them to the website. The integration can match the lead with a Google Ads campaign, giving a more complete picture of the lead's behavior and preferences. Users can then use this information to create targeted campaigns or personalize communication when reaching out to the lead.

3. Increased Data Visibility

The integration offers users a 360-degree view of customer data, including Google Ads metrics that show ROI, impressions, clicks, and other important campaign data. Users can filter data by different parameters, such as products and campaigns, to analyze the performance of different marketing initiatives more closely. The information helps users improve their targeting and content strategies, leading to better conversions and customer engagement.

Conclusion

Integrating Google Ads and SugarCRM 7+ through API or SDK provides numerous benefits for enterprise sales and marketing teams. Teams get a holistic view of customer data and can manage their campaigns and leads more effectively, leading to improved ROI and engagement. With the integration, teams can streamline workflows and save time, reducing errors and improving efficiency.
