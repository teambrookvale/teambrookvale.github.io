Integrating GoTo Webinar and BigCommerce for a Better E-Commerce Experience

As more businesses switch to e-commerce, having the right tools to manage online sales and customer engagement is essential. Two popular platforms that are commonly used are GoTo Webinar and BigCommerce. In this post, we’ll explore how integrating the two platforms using API or SDK can solve some common problems faced by e-commerce stores today.

What is GoTo Webinar?

GoTo Webinar is a versatile platform for hosting and managing online webinars, virtual events, and online meetings. It provides numerous features such as screen sharing, interactive polls, Q&A sessions, and more to make online events more engaging and interactive. Hosts can organize registration, send invitations, and view analytics of their events to monitor the engagement metrics and plan more successful events accordingly.

What is BigCommerce?

BigCommerce is a leading e-commerce platform that allows businesses to set up online stores quickly and easily. It has numerous features, such as product management, order processing, payment integrations, and more to handle all aspects of online sales. It also has themes and plugins to customize the store’s design and functionality, making it easy to create unique and compelling online stores.

Integrating GoTo Webinar with BigCommerce

By integrating GoTo Webinar with BigCommerce, e-commerce stores can enjoy an enhanced customer experience by organizing webinars and virtual events. This integration can be done using API or SDK, which allows for real-time data synchronization between the two platforms and enables hosts to manage their events seamlessly from the main dashboard.

Problems Their Integration Solves

1. Boosting engagement and sales

With this integration, online stores can promote their products and services through webinars and virtual events. They can invite their existing customers to attend and reach out to new potential customers by promoting their events on social media channels. When customers have the chance to engage with the brand through an interactive webinar, it boosts the likelihood of making a purchase.

2. Improving target marketing

By attending a webinar, customers are expressing an explicit interest in a brand’s products or services, indicating a higher likelihood of becoming a paying customer. Storeowners can use this information to refine their target marketing efforts by providing more tailored content to the attendees of their webinars and thereby increasing the chances of capturing more paying customers.

Conclusion

Integrating GoTo Webinar with BigCommerce can offer online stores many new opportunities to boost engagement, sales, and loyalty. It can be an effective marketing strategy, enabling small businesses to compete with larger retailers and reach out to an audience that may not have been reachable otherwise. Its easy-to-use interface and real-time synchronization make it a practical solution for businesses looking to capitalize on the benefits of virtual events and webinars. If you have not already done so, now is the time to explore the benefits of integrating these two solutions to upgrade your e-commerce store’s operations.
