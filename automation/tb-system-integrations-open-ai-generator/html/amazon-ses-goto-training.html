Integrating Amazon SES and GoToTraining for Streamlined Communication

In today's digital age, communication is key to running a successful business. Thus, many companies leverage a variety of tools to streamline their communication process. Two popular tools for this purpose are Amazon SES (Simple Email Service) and GoToTraining. Both enable businesses to communicate with large groups of people, but in different ways. Amazon SES helps businesses automate their email marketing campaigns, while GoToTraining enables them to host virtual training sessions.

Why Integrate Amazon SES and GoToTraining?

The answer to this question is quite simple. Integrating Amazon SES and GoToTraining means having a streamlined communication process. When a business uses Amazon SES, they can easily send out emails for marketing campaigns or newsletters. However, if they want to send out custom training invitations, they have to use another tool like GoToTraining. Integrating the two tools means that businesses can send both kinds of messages from one platform and easily track which recipients open and engage with the content.

How to Integrate Amazon SES and GoToTraining

Integrating Amazon SES and GoToTraining can be done through API or SDK. The following steps outline how to integrate Amazon SES with GoToTraining:

  1. Login to your AWS account and navigate to SES.
  2. Click on "Email Addresses" to verify your email address.
  3. Create an IAM user on your AWS account and assign them the 'AmazonSESFullAccess' policy.
  4. Take note of your Access Key ID and Secret Access Key.
  5. Login to your GoToTraining account and click on 'Administration'.
  6. Select 'Integrations' and then 'Add Integrations'.
  7. Select Amazon SES from the list of options and input your Access Key ID and Secret Access Key.
  8. Select the email templates and the frequency with which you want to send messages.
  9. Voila! You have now integrated Amazon SES and GoToTraining.

Problems Integration Solves

Integrating Amazon SES and GoToTraining solves many problems businesses face. First, it saves time by enabling businesses to send both marketing and training emails from one platform. It also enables companies to better track engagement with messages, leading to better insights for targeted messages. Finally, integrating the two tools leads to streamlined communication, driving greater efficiency and productivity.

Conclusion

Amazon SES and GoToTraining are two popular communication tools that can work great on their own. But, integrating them means having more control over your communication process, better insights, and improved efficiency. By following the above-given steps, businesses can easily integrate Amazon SES and GoToTraining and enjoy streamlined communication.
