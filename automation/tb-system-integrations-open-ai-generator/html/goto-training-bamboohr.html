Integrating GoTo Training and BambooHR

As a company, it can be challenging to manage your employees' training and performance across multiple platforms. Fortunately, with the integration of GoTo Training and BambooHR, this can now be done with ease.

GoTo Training

GoTo Training is an online training software that allows companies to create and deliver virtual training sessions for their employees. It offers features such as screen sharing, recording, and engagement tools to make training sessions interactive and engaging.

BambooHR

BambooHR is a human resources management software that enables companies to streamline their HR processes. It offers features like employee onboarding, performance management, and time-off tracking, all in one place.

Integration through API or SDK

The integration between GoTo Training and BambooHR can be done through their APIs or SDKs. Through these integration options, employee performance data can be synced with the training data to provide insights into how training affects employee performance.

Problems their integration solves

The integration of GoTo Training and BambooHR solves several problems for companies, including:

  • Combining employee training records with performance data to get a comprehensive overview of employee development.
  • Organizing and automating training sessions and scheduling in one integrated system.
  • Eliminating redundant data entry across multiple platforms.

Conclusion

Integrating GoTo Training and BambooHR may seem like a small change, but it has significant impacts that benefit both employees and the company. By combining training and performance management, companies can get a comprehensive view of employee development and optimize their training programs to achieve better results.
