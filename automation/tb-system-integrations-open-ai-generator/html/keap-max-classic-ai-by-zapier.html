What is Keap Max Classic?

Keap Max Classic is a CRM (customer relationship management) software that aims to streamline and automate a small business's administration tasks. It offers features such as appointment scheduling, contact management, email marketing, and sales pipeline management. The goal of Keap Max Classic is to reduce the time businesses spend on repetitive tasks so that they can focus on more crucial aspects of their operations.

What is AI by Zapier?

AI by Zapier is a powerful tool that enhances automation capabilities by using artificial intelligence. It allows one to create more complex workflows that can analyze data, recognize patterns, and learn from previous tasks. Through AI by Zapier, one can set up automated processes like never before, effectively bringing an army of virtual assistants to your aid.
