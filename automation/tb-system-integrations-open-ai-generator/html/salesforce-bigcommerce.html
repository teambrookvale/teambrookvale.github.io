What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based software platform that offers a wide range of applications and services designed to help businesses manage their customer relationships and data effectively. It provides a comprehensive set of tools for all areas of customer management, including sales, service, marketing, and analytics.

What is BigCommerce?

BigCommerce is a popular eCommerce platform that allows businesses to build and manage their online stores. With features like customizable templates, secure payments, and built-in SEO, BigCommerce makes it easy to create a professional-looking website and start selling products online.

Integration of Salesforce and BigCommerce through API or SDK

The integration of Salesforce and BigCommerce through API or SDK is a powerful solution for eCommerce businesses. It allows you to streamline your operations and automate many of your processes, so you can save time and focus on growing your business.

With the integration, you can:

  • Synchronize customer data between the two platforms, so you have a complete view of your customers and their orders.
  • Automatically create and update leads, contacts, and opportunities in Salesforce based on customer information in BigCommerce.
  • Gain insights into your sales and marketing activities through built-in analytics and reporting in Salesforce.
  • Track your inventory and orders in real-time across both platforms.

The Problems their Integration Solves

The integration of Salesforce and BigCommerce solves many common problems faced by eCommerce businesses:

  • Manual data entry:

    • Without integration, you may have to manually enter customer data into both platforms, which can be time-consuming and error-prone.

  • Lack of visibility:

    • Without integration, you may not have a complete view of your customers, which can lead to missed opportunities and lost revenue.

  • Difficulty in tracking inventory:

    • Without integration, you may have to manually track inventory levels across both platforms, which can lead to overstocking or stockouts.

  • Suboptimal marketing efforts:

    • Without integration, it can be difficult to track your marketing efforts and see which campaigns are driving sales.
