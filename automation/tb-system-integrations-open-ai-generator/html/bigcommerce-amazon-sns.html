Integrating BigCommerce with Amazon SNS with API/SDK

BigCommerce and Amazon SNS are two powerful platforms that provide a range of benefits to businesses. What if you could integrate the two platforms to enhance your online store's sales and customer experience? Well, now you can with the use of Application Programming Interface (API) or Software Development Kit (SDK). In this post, we will cover how to achieve that and the benefits of doing so.

What is BigCommerce?

BigCommerce is a leading e-commerce platform that provides online businesses with a website, shopping cart, and payment processing capabilities. It is an all-in-one solution for small and large businesses to sell their products and services online. With BigCommerce, merchants can design a storefront that reflects their business and brand, manage their store's orders and inventory, and access various analytics and reports.

What is Amazon SNS?

Amazon Simple Notification Service (SNS) is a web service that enables businesses to send messages and notifications to subscribed recipients. It provides a flexible, scalable, and cost-effective way to send SMS, email, and push notifications to customers, employees, and other recipients. Amazon SNS integrates with various services, including AWS Lambda, Amazon SQS, and Amazon EC2, to trigger actions and workflows when a message is received. With SNS, you can deliver timely and relevant messages to your customers and users.

Integration with API/SDK

Through the use of an API or SDK, you can integrate BigCommerce and Amazon SNS. It can be done in a few simple steps:

  1. Register for an Amazon SNS account and obtain your SNS Access Keys.
  2. Log in to your BigCommerce account and navigate to the Settings > API Account Details page.
  3. Click the "Create a V2/V3 API Token" button to generate a token.
  4. Navigate to the Settings > Store Settings > Webhooks page.
  5. Add a new webhook and specify the SNS endpoint URL and provide an SNS topic ARN.
  6. Save your changes and test the integration.

Problems solved

The integration of BigCommerce and Amazon SNS solves several problems that online businesses may encounter. By integrating the two platforms, you can:

  • Automate your engagement and push notifications to your customers
  • Track and manage your stock levels and orders more effectively
  • Improve your customer experience by enabling real-time updates and actions
  • Reduce manual tasks and errors by automating key workflows
