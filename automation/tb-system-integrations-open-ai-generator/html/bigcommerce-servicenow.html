BigCommerce and ServiceNow Integration: A Comprehensive Guide

BigCommerce is a leading ecommerce platform that enables businesses to create and operate their online stores. On the other hand, ServiceNow is a cloud-based IT service management tool that streamlines and automates various IT activities. While both offer essential features to businesses, integrating the two can help elevate the overall operations of a brand.

Integrating BigCommerce and ServiceNow

Integrating BigCommerce with ServiceNow is possible through an API or SDK. An API (Application Programming Interface) is a set of protocols and tools for building software, while an SDK (Software Development Kit) provides developers with a set of tools to support app development.

The integration of the two platforms enables improved communication and streamlined operational flow. When a customer places an order on your BigCommerce store, the order data is automatically transferred to ServiceNow, which helps track the order fulfillment process. Conversely, on the ServiceNow platform, customer service agents can see all customer orders, transaction history, and support tickets- providing them with the information needed to offer timely and informed assistance to customers.

Solving Business Problems with BigCommerce and ServiceNow Integration

The integration of BigCommerce and ServiceNow aids businesses in various ways:

Improved Efficiency

With the integration, businesses can automate tedious manual tasks, such as data entry. This, in turn, frees up time for business owners to focus on more important activities like growing their business and boosting revenue.

Enhanced Customer Experience

Customers value quick and efficient customer service. The integration of the two platforms enables businesses to offer prompt assistance by providing customer service representatives access to important customer data that they otherwise wouldn't have. This allows businesses to understand the customer's needs and deliver a more personalized experience.

Increased Productivity

By providing employees with a platform that offers real-time data and information about inventory, orders, and customer history, businesses can see an increase in productivity and fewer errors. As a result, employees can focus on more important responsibilities, and the likelihood of customer complaints decreases.

Conclusion

The integration of BigCommerce and ServiceNow can significantly improve business operations and the overall customer experience. Employing the two platforms together allows businesses to leverage their strengths to address their respective challenges, thus boosting productivity, efficiency, and revenue. The integration of the two systems eliminates the need for manual data entry, saves time and energy, and reduces errors, among other benefits.
